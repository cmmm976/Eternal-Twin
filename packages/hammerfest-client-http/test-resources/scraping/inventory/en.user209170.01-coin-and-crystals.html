fridge

The Fridge is the place where Igor keeps his collection of items. The items can be obtained throughout the game in the Adventure mode. Usually you need to obtain 10 times the item before being allowed to keep it in the Fridge.

When an item is unblocked, it becomes visible for everybody in your public sheet and you are able to use it in the forum!
